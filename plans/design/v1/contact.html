Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register to vote?

You can register to vote by visiting our registration page and completing the online form. You'll need to verify your identity through our secure authentication process.

Is my vote secure?

Absolutely. We use post-quantum cryptographic algorithms, zero-knowledge proofs, and anonymous routing to ensure your vote is completely secure and private. Learn more on our security page.

How can I verify my vote was counted?

After submitting your vote, you'll receive a unique vote ID and cryptographic receipt. You can use this to verify your vote was recorded correctly through our blockchain-based audit trail. Visit our features page for more details.

What if I need help during the voting process?

Our support team is available during all election periods. You can reach us through the contact form, live chat (available on election days), or by calling our support hotline. We also offer accessibility support for voters with disabilities.
